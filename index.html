What is ISSA?

Information system security association (ISSA) is the student club of NIE whose primary focus is cyber security and deals with bridging it with the state-of-the-art technologies.

Why join ISSA?

We aim to improve and encourage the growth of students in various technological domains. Here at ISSA, we focus on various technical, vocational and recreational activities dealing with computer science, information technology and information security. We provide several opportunities for students to showcase their expertise through a number of contests and also help atudents to learn technology the practical way.
